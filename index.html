Scrobbyl

Scratch n' Share

Scrobbyl makes it easy to keep Last.fm up-to-date on what you're listening to on vinyl.

How does it work?

Scrobbyl uses the line-in port on your computer to listen to what's playing on your turntable. It periodically takes a sonic footprint of the music and identifies the track info using the Echo Nest API. It then scrobbles the track to your Last.fm account.
